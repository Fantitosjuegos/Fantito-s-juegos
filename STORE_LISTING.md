\## Full Description (max 4000 chars — Google Play)

Fantito's Juegos is the AI-powered party card game that generates questions

made for YOUR exact group, tonight.



Not generic truth-or-dare. Not the same cards every time.

Fantito reads your group — who you are, how you vibe, what scene you're in —

and generates 25 cards built for this exact moment.



🎮 HOW IT WORKS

\- Add your players and set your relationships

\- Choose your vibe: chill, wild, flirty, deep, or chaotic

\- Pick your scene: house party, bar, road trip, pregame, vacation

\- Fantito's AI generates 25 cards made for your group



🃏 25 CARDS PER GAME

Every deck is unique. Questions, dares, votes, quizzes, pair observations,

hot takes, mini-games, charades and more — all adapted to your group in real time.



🌍 7 LANGUAGES

English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Arabic (with RTL support)



🔒 ALWAYS SAFE

Built-in safety system adapts to your game mode:

\- Family mode: clean, wholesome, fun for all ages

\- Normal mode: balanced chaos and laughs

\- 18+ mode: spicy but always consensual, never dangerous



✨ FEATURES

\- AI-generated cards unique to your group

\- 7 languages including Arabic

\- Multiple game modes (Family, Normal, 18+)

\- Consumption-aware (sober, drinkers, mixed)

\- Scene-aware (house party, bar, road trip, and more)

\- Anti-repeat system — never see the same card twice

\- Works offline for card display (AI requires connection)
